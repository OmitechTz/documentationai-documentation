## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account verification
- No credit card required for the free 7-day trial
</Callout>

Gemba Write is a web-based platform, so you can start immediately without downloads or installations.

## Sign Up for Your Free 7-Day Trial

Create your account in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Platform" icon="globe">
    Go to [https://gembawrite.com](https://gembawrite.com) and click **Start Writing Now** or **Free 7-Day Trial**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and select your role (e.g., Researcher, PhD Student).
    
    Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer a few quick questions about your research field to personalize your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
Your account activates instantly. Cancel anytime during the trial.
</Callout>

## Create and Access Your Workspace

Workspaces organize your manuscripts and collaborate with teams.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Workspace" icon="plus">
    Log in at [https://app.gembawrite.com](https://app.gembawrite.com).
    
    Click **New Workspace** and name it (e.g., "Clinical Trial Manuscript").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite Collaborators" icon="users">
    Optionally, share the workspace invite link with co-authors via email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Select your workspace to enter the live quality dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigate the Live Quality Dashboard

The dashboard provides real-time feedback on quality score, citations, and structure.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Quality Score" icon="activity">
    View your manuscript's overall score (e.g., 94%) and breakdown:
    
    - Coverage: Source completeness
    - Structure: Section alignment
    - Gaps: Missing citations or logic
    
    Issues highlight in real-time as you draft.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Citations Panel" icon="book-open">
    Search 200M+ sources. Add verified DOIs:
    
    ```markdown
    @article{doe2023,
      title = {Sample Research Title},
      author = {Doe, John},
      journal = {Nature},
      year = {2023},
      doi = {10.1038/s41586-023-00001-2}
    }
    ```
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Outline View" icon="list">
    Drag-and-drop sections to build structure aligned to your research question.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Draft Your First Outline

Start structuring your manuscript.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outline Editor" icon="edit-3">
    In your workspace, click **New Manuscript** > **Outline**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Sections" icon="plus-square">
    Use the structured drafting tools:
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,LaTeX">
    ````markdown
    # Introduction
    
    ## Research Question
    State your hypothesis here.
    
    ## Literature Review
    Cite 3-5 key sources.
    
    # Methods
    ````
    ````latex
    \section{Introduction}
    
    \subsection{Research Question}
    State your hypothesis here.
    
    \subsection{Literature Review}
    Cite 3-5 key sources.
    
    \section{Methods}
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate with AI" icon="zap">
    Click **AI Assist** to auto-fill sections based on your topic.
  </Step>
</Steps>
