---
title: Can't log in to documentation portal
description: Fix login problems for docs.gembawrite.com
---

## Symptoms
- Login page refreshes, no error shown
- "Invalid credentials" message appears

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Ensure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and follow instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Log out, clear cookies, then sign in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If issues persist, contact [Support](mailto:support@omitechgroup.com).
