## Overview

Gemba Write empowers researchers to create publication-ready manuscripts without rework. You draft, cite, and export in one seamless flow while receiving real-time quality feedback on coverage, structure, and gaps. Trusted by over 12,000 researchers at top universities like MIT, Stanford, and Oxford, Gemba Write ensures zero defects slip into your submissions.

With a 94% average quality score across 12,450+ papers, you benefit from auto-verified citations across 200M+ sources, AI-assisted structured drafting, and journal-ready exports.
